# Child Care (Developmental Nannies)
Overview
KEEL Child Care professionals provide reliable home-based care for young children while supporting their developmental needs. Our caregivers focus on safety, routine, and engaging activities that help children build confidence and independence.

What Our Child Care Professionals Do
- Supervise and ensure the child’s safety and well-being
- Support daily routines such as meals, playtime, and rest
- Engage children in learning-based play and activities
- Help build communication and social interaction skills
- Encourage developmental milestones through structured play

Training
All KEEL caregivers complete a basic training program covering:
- child development basics
- safety and supervision
- positive behaviour guidance
- communication with young children

Verification
Every caregiver is:
- identity verified
- background checked
- trained in child safety protocols

Availability
Caregivers are matched based on location proximity and commute independently to the family’s home.
